(JVM, COMMON) CalendarDate(year: Year, month: Month, dayInYear: DayInYear, dayInMonth: DayInMonth, dayOfWeek: DayOfWeek)

A class that represents a Date within a Calendar with no reference to exact time, such as, minutes or hours, or a region. Note multiple CalendarDates may be considered equal if their values are the same, even though the Moments they came from may be in different regions, so technically they do not represent the same time. This is because a CalendarDate has no reference to a region.
