utc

(JVM, COMMON) @ExperimentalTime fun TimeProvider.utc(): Moment

Retrieves the Moment that represents the unix epoch time, or the time that has elapsed at UTC since the epoch (January 1, 1970 midnight UTC/GMT, not counting leap seconds in ISO 8601:1970-01-01T00:00:00Z). The region for this time is static at UTC/GMT.
