isGregorianLeapYear

(JVM, COMMON) val isGregorianLeapYear: Boolean

Determines whether this Year is a leap year according to the Gregorian Calendar. According to this system, the average year is about 365.2425 days long and spaces leap years every four years. The rules for determining this value are:

"Every year that is exactly divisible by four is a leap year, except for years that are exactly divisible by 100, but these centurial years are leap years if they are exactly divisible by 400."
